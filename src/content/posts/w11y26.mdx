I am documenting my journey towards **Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE) by age 45**. Turning 25 soon gives me a 20-year runway. As the adage goes, *"What we cannot measure, we cannot improve."* Instead of seeing FIRE as a single finish line, I view it as a series of milestones. My strategy is to hit **Lean**, **Coast**, and **Barista** FIRE as checkpoints on the way to my ultimate goal: **Fat FIRE**.

---

## The Math: Accounting for 2046

To keep these goals realistic, we have to account for Singapore's inflation. Historically, headline inflation has averaged **2.1%**, but I am using **2.5%** for a safer margin.

To calculate what a lifestyle costs 20 years from now, we use the **Future Value (FV)** formula:

<div align="center">
  $$FV = PV \times (1 + r)^n$$
</div>

* **PV**: Today's monthly cost
* **r**: 2.5% inflation
* **n**: 20 years
* **The 3% Rule**: Since I plan to be retired for 50 years, I use a **3% Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR)** instead of the usual 4%.

## My FIRE Progression Tiers

I’ve categorized my goals into four distinct phases. Each phase represents a new level of freedom.

| Goal | Today's Cost | 2046 Monthly Cost | 2046 FIRE Number |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Lean FIRE** | ~$2,000 | ~$3,277 | **~$1,310,000** |
| **2. Coast FIRE** | Varies | See Note | **Projected Growth** |
| **3. Barista FIRE**| ~$3,500 | ~$5,735 | **~$2,294,000** |
| **4. Fat FIRE** | ~$10,000 | ~$16,386 | **~$6,550,000** |

---

### Milestone 1: Lean FIRE (The Safety Net)
**The Goal:** ~$1.31M
**Lifestyle:** "Survival Mode." Covers HDB conservancy fees, public transport, and hawker meals. This is the point where I am technically free, but with zero frills.

### Milestone 2: Coast FIRE (The Mental Shift)
**The Goal:** A specific "Invested Amount" today.
**Lifestyle:** I continue working my high-intensity tech/dev jobs, but I **stop** contributing to my portfolio. I let time and compounding do the work. This is where the stress of "saving" disappears.

### Milestone 3: Barista FIRE (The Semi-Retirement)
**The Goal:** ~$2.29M
**Lifestyle:** I quit the corporate grind. My portfolio covers my basics, and I work a lower-stress job (perhaps freelance web dev or teaching) to cover "fun" expenses or healthcare.

### Milestone 4: Fat FIRE (The Ultimate Dream)
**The Goal:** ~$6.55M
**Lifestyle:** The "Singapore Dream." Private property, car ownership (COE included), and no-compromise travel. This is the final destination of my 20-year journey.

---

> **Note on Sequence of Returns:** Retiring at 45 means I face 50 years of market ups and downs. By aiming for these mini-goals, I build psychological resilience. Even if I don't hit "Fat FIRE" by 45, hitting "Coast" or "Lean" earlier provides a massive safety buffer.
